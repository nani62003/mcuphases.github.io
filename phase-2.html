PHASE-2

Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) phase-2

Phase 2 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) continued the
interconnected superhero saga, introducing new characters and
deepening the storyline. It comprised six movies and was a critical
stepping stone toward the monumental events of Phase 3.





  • 1. Iron Man 3 (2013) - Tony Stark faces a powerful terrorist


    called the Mandarin while dealing with the aftermath of the


    events in "The Avengers."






  • 2. Thor: The Dark World (2013) - Thor must team up with his


    mischievous brother Loki to stop the dark elf Malekith from


    unleashing a destructive weapon called the Aether.





  • 3. Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014) - Captain America discovers


    a deadly conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. and faces a formidable assassin


    known as the Winter Soldier, who has a surprising connection to his past.





  • 4. Guardians of the Galaxy (2014) - A group of misfits, led by


    Peter Quill/Star-Lord, comes together to stop the power-hungry


    Ronan the Accuser from using an ancient artifact called the Orb.





  • 5. Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015) - The Avengers reunite to confront


    Ultron, an AI program created by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner, which


    turns against humanity, leading to an epic battle to save Earth.





  • 6. Ant-Man (2015) - Scott Lang, a master thief, takes on the mantle


    of Ant-Man under the guidance of Hank Pym to prevent a dangerous


    technology from falling into the wrong hands.








Phase 2 delves deeper into the personal struggles of the heroes
and explores new corners of the Marvel Universe, including the
cosmic realm with the Guardians of the Galaxy. It lays the groundwork
for major storylines that culminate in Phase 3 and beyond.






Each movie in Phase 2 contributed to the overall narrative of the MCU,
introducing new characters, storylines, and conflicts, and building up
to significant events that would shape the future phases of the
Marvel Cinematic Universe.
